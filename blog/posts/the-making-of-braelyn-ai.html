The Making of Braelyn.ai

December 20, 2025

The first time I visited Berlin, I stepped off the plane and thought, "This is it. This is my identity." The further east I went, the more I resonated with the people, the culture, the music, the grit. More than anywhere else, discovering Berghain changed my life.
Berghain has become a regular pilgrimage in my life. Every trip I've made offers a new perspective on my life and a new understanding of the world. When I decided to build this site, I wanted it to be meticulously designed to reflect who I am through the language of a 3D space. The visitor of the site within seconds should be able to develop a reasonable understanding of my identity.
